Connections between the Raspberry pi pico w and the peripherals of the v2 prototype:

Stepper motor drivers:
D1 – 0		left motor direction
S1 – 1		left motor step
D2 – 2		right motor direction
S2 – 3		right motor step

Shift register:
O – 6		latch out
S – 7		shift on
I – 8		shift input

Servo pwm:
Spwm – 15	RC pwm signal

Ultrasonic sensor:
T – 16		trigger
E – 17		echo

Line sensors:
L0 – A2		right
L1 – A1		middle
L2 – A0		left
