Connections between the Raspberry pi pico w and the peripherals of the v2 prototype:

Stepper motor drivers:
D1 	– 2		left front motor direction
S1 	– 3		left front motor step
D2 	– 4		right front motor direction
S2 	– 5		right front motor step
D1 	– 6		left back motor direction
S1 	– 7		left back motor step
D2 	– 8		right back motor direction
S2 	– 9		right back motor step

Shift register:
O 	– 18		latch out
S 	– 19		shift on
I 	– 20		shift input


Line sensors:
L0 	– A2		right
L1 	– A1		middle
L2 	– A0		left
VCC 	– 3.3v
